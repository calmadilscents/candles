Kapampangan Goddess

The ancient Kapampangans believed that the goddess of the seas was Indung Laut, known as Mother Ocean and Naga Lakandanum was the first serpent to swim in the Primeval Ocean. Their union brought forth Lakandanum, the God of Water named after his father. His twin brother is Lakanapi, the God of Fire.
